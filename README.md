## 💧 JalTantra – Water Technical NOC System

> ⚡ Transforming water technical approvals into a transparent, automated, and efficient digital workflow system.

### 📌 Overview

**JalTantra** is a government-based web application developed to digitize and automate the **Water Technical NOC approval process** for construction projects.

The system handles both **Commencement Certificate (CC)** and **Occupancy Certificate (OC)** workflows, ensuring a structured approval process with technical scrutiny, payments, and final NOC issuance.

---

### 🚀 Key Features

* 📄 Online Application Submission (CC & OC)
* 🔁 Multi-Level Authority Workflow
* 🔍 Technical Scrutiny & Verification Process
* 💳 Scrutiny Fee Demand & Payment Integration
* 📊 Dashboard & Analytical Reports
* 🔗 API-Based Data Autofetch (BPMS)
* 📑 Receipt Generation System
* 🔍 Real-Time Status Tracking
* 📥 Final NOC Upload & Management

---

### 🏗️ Modules

#### 🔹 Commencement Certificate (CC)

* Application Submission with Water Details
* Site Inspection by Authority
* Scrutiny & Verification Process
* Fee Demand & Online Payment
* Final NOC Approval

#### 🔹 Occupancy Certificate (OC)

* Linked with CC Data (Auto-fetch)
* Multi-Level Authority Review
* Final Verification & Scrutiny
* Payment & Receipt Generation
* Final NOC Upload

---

### ⚙️ Workflow

#### 🔹 CC Flow

1. Login to BPMS Portal
2. Select ULB & Project
3. Choose Water Technical NOC
4. Fill Application & Water Details
5. Submit Application
6. Site Inspection by Authority
7. Verification & Scrutiny Fee Demand
8. Online Payment
9. Receipt Generation
10. Final Review & NOC Upload
11. Process Completion

#### 🔹 OC Flow

1. Login & Select Project
2. Choose NOC Service
3. Fill Application Details
4. Submit Application
5. Multi-Level Review
6. Scrutiny & Fee Demand
7. Payment & Receipt
8. Final NOC Upload
9. Process Completion
